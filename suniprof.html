• Sunidhi Chauhan:

    •           Sunidhi Chauhan is one of India's most renowned playback singers, known for her versatile voice and powerful performances. She was born on August 14, 1983, in Delhi, India. Sunidhi’s musical career started at a very young age, and she gained national fame in her teenage years.

             She made her Bollywood playback singing debut with the song "Lak Lak" from the movie Shastra (1996), but it was her song "Ruki Ruki Si Zindagi" from Mast (1999) that brought her into the spotlight. Since then, she has sung a vast array of songs across genres, from romantic ballads to high-energy dance numbers.

              Throughout her career, Sunidhi has won numerous awards, including Filmfare Awards and IIFA Awards. She’s worked with several top composers and singers in the industry, and her voice has made her a go-to choice for several hit songs in Hindi cinema. Beyond Bollywood, she has also worked in regional films and has been part of various music reality shows, both as a judge and mentor, which further showcases her popularity and respect in the music industry.
